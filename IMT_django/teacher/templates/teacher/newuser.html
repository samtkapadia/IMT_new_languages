Interactive Machine Teaching

This is an automated system to help a learner read and pronounce words in the Czech language.

A few different teaching algorithms are being assessed. Please refrain from taking this test if you are already familiar with the Czech, Slovak or Polish languages.

After clicking Start, you will have an opportunity to practice recording your voice.

Following that, the teaching will commence. Here, you will be shown a sequence of Czech words. For each word, record your pronunciation using the controls on the page. You will then be given feedback on your answer, and a correct pronunciation which you can listen to.

Please note that the recordings you provide will be stored for 6 months and then deleted. You can leave the experiment anytime by closing the browser session.

After the teaching rounds there will be some testing rounds. The entire course should take approx. 10 minutes to complete.

Please press Start below to begin.
